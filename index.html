San Jose Sharks

The San Jose Sharks are an NHL team based in San Jose, California and were the Western Conference Champions of the 2015-16 season.

Key Players

Some of the Sharks' star players helped them achieve the best season in franchise history.

  • Joe Thornton, center
  • Joe Pavelski, center
  • Logan Couture, center
  • Brent Burns, defenseman
  • Martin Jones, goaltender
